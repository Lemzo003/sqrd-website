Temporary works & structural engineering

Temporary works and structural design that adapts to your requirement, TW category and programme.

Fees and delivery time are always confirmed individually, based on the type of temporary works, complexity and urgency. You receive a tailored proposal for each project.

BS 5975 • Eurocodes • Vendor guidance • Practical, buildable engineering

How delivery works

Each request is reviewed and scoped with you. Delivery depends on:

  • Temporary Works category (BS 5975)
  • Complexity of the structure
  • Quality of information provided
  • Required programme / urgency

A written confirmation email is always issued, including scope, assumptions, fee and indicative delivery before work starts.

Temporary works services (ready to use)

A clear menu of services you can request directly in your email brief.

  • Formwork and falsework design for slabs, walls, beams and rafts
  • Backpropping and re-shoring layouts and checks
  • Temporary propping of RC walls, cores and frames
  • Retaining / kingpost solutions and excavation support checks
  • Working platforms and access platforms (including MEWP access)
  • Scaffold platforms and birdcages (TG20 / supplier-based)
  • Hoist landing platforms and tie arrangements
  • Edge protection to steel and concrete structures
  • Lifting beams, spreader frames and A-frames for modules / precast units
  • Precast element lifting and transport checks
  • Crane mats and working platform assessments
  • Local RC slab / foundation checks for concentrated loads
  • Steel member checks and local strengthening details

When you send a brief, you can simply refer to these items (for example: “Working platform for MEWP – see service list, item 5”).

Process

Simple, repeatable steps that fit your temporary works procedure.

  • 1. Send TW brief
    Drawings, loads, standards, supplier systems and any target dates.
  • 2. Proposal issued
    You receive a short email confirming scope, assumptions, fee and indicative delivery.
  • 3. Design & checks completed
    Calculations, sketches and notes produced in line with BS 5975 and relevant Eurocodes.
  • 4. Issue for your TW procedure
    Packs are issued for your internal TWC / CAT 2 / CAT 3 review and site implementation.
Standards & tools
  • BS 5975 temporary works procedures and permissible stress
  • Relevant Eurocodes and National Annexes
  • Supplier data (scaffold, formwork, anchors, systems)
  • Autodesk Robot Structural Analysis plus hand calculations and spreadsheets

Final sign-off, TW categories and permits remain under your organisation’s procedures.
