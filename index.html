Specialist Temporary Works design & independent checks for construction projects.

Clear, buildable Temporary Works solutions aligned with BS 5975 and relevant Eurocodes, tailored to real site constraints and programme.

What to send to start quickly
Copy this list into your email enquiry so your request can be scoped fast.

Minimum information

  • TW item(s) required (from the services list)
  • Sketch / drawing / photos
  • Loads, geometry, and key constraints
  • Required timescale / deadline

Helpful (if available)

  • Ground info / bearing capacity (if relevant)
  • Preferred system / supplier data
  • Any review requirements (Cat 2 / Cat 3)
  • Budget guidance (optional)
Fees (simple)
Fees are agreed per enquiry and depend on the requirements, the number/complexity of items and the programme.
  • TW category / risk level
  • Complexity and number of TW items
  • Information provided at the start
  • Urgency and review requirements
