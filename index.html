What to send

Fast proposals require clear inputs.

Drawings / sketches

GA / sections / details + any site constraints (access, foundations, limits).

Loads & use

Concrete pour heights/rate (if relevant), imposed loads, plant, wheel loads, etc.

Programme

Required issue date, urgency (standard / fast-track), revision expectations.

Scope boundary

Temporary Works only. If information is insufficient, a Feasibility Review may be proposed first.

Fees & Proposals

Fees are confirmed after drawings and scope review.

How fees are set

Temporary Works pricing depends on scope, complexity, risk category (CAT 2 / CAT 3), programme, and the quality of information provided. A written proposal is issued once the TW item and inputs are reviewed.

Typical scope

Single Temporary Works Item

  • Calculations (PDF) with assumptions
  • Buildable sketch / mark-ups
  • Design certificate (as applicable)
  • Revisions agreed in the proposal

Examples: formwork, propping, platforms, hoist landing checks.

Higher risk

Design + Independent Check

  • Full TW design package
  • Independent check (CAT 2 / CAT 3)
  • Certificate & holding points
  • Issue pack suitable for site

Recommended for high-risk / high-value temporary works.

Urgent

Fast-Track / Emergency

  • Turnaround depends on scope and inputs
  • Priority slot (subject to availability)
  • Clear issue timeline in the proposal
  • Out-of-hours support (if required)

Confirmed only once drawings and constraints are reviewed.

If information is incomplete, a short paid feasibility review may be proposed first to confirm feasibility, key risks, constraints and next steps. Any fee and deliverable will be stated clearly in the proposal.
