How the Overnight Process Works

A simple, transparent workflow that fits around your existing project teams and procedures.

  • 1
    Send your brief (late afternoon / evening).
    You share drawings, sketches, loads and any standards or supplier systems to be used.
  • 2
    Scope & fee agreed.
    You receive a short confirmation email with deliverables, assumptions and a fixed or hourly fee.
  • 3
    Overnight engineering.
    Design, checks and documentation are prepared while your office is closed.
  • 4
    Delivery before your morning.
    You receive calculation notes and supporting sketches ready for internal review, TW checks and issue.
