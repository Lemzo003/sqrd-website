What to send

Fast proposals require clear inputs.

Drawings / sketches

GA / sections / details + any site constraints (access, foundations, limits).

Loads & use

Concrete pour heights/rate (if relevant), imposed loads, plant, wheel loads, etc.

Programme

Required issue date, urgency (standard / fast-track), revision expectations.

Scope boundary

Temporary Works only. If information is insufficient, a Feasibility Review may be proposed first.

Fees & Proposals

Fees are confirmed after drawings and scope review.

How fees are set

Temporary Works fees depend on scope, complexity, risk category (CAT 2 / CAT 3), programme and the quality of information provided. A written proposal is issued once drawings and requirements are reviewed.
