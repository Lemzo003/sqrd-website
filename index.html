How the process works

A simple, transparent workflow that can fit into your existing temporary works procedure.

  • 1
    Send your temporary works brief.
    Drawings, sketches, loads, applicable standards and supplier systems.
  • 2
    Scope, delivery and fee agreed.
    You receive a short confirmation email with deliverables, assumptions, target delivery and fee.
  • 3
    Design and checks carried out.
    Calculations and sketches are prepared in line with the agreed scope and standards.
  • 4
    Issue for your internal TW review.
    Packs are issued for your internal category check and sign-off route.
